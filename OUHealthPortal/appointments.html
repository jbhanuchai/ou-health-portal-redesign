Appointment Notes

  • New appointments open daily at 8:00 AM.
  • Please arrive 15 minutes before your appointment time.
  • Appointments after 4 PM must check in by 4 PM.
  • Telehealth visits are only available for patients currently in Oklahoma.
  • A $20 no-show fee may apply for missed appointments not cancelled at least 1 hour in advance.
